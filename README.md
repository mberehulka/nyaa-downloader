# Nyaa downloader

Find any anime in your  watching list and open the torrent file found in , and mark the episode as watched

# Usage

- Login into your [AniList](https://www.anilist.co) account in the window that shows up after starting the APP
- Add some anime to your watching list
- Select the anime (move with arrows, WASD keys or mouse scroll) and click Space or Enter:
<img src="./assets/screenshots/1.png" width="100%"/>
- The programm will search [Nyaa](https://www.nyaa.si) for a torrent in 1080p of the next episode
- Select one of the results and click hit Space or Enter
<img src="./assets/screenshots/2.png" width="100%"/>
- The .torrent file will open in your default torrent APP
<img src="./assets/screenshots/3.png" width="100%"/>
- After that the episode will be marked as watched in your watching list
